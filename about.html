Our heritage

Where Craftsmanship Meets Modern Luxury

Marias Handbag was founded in Atlanta, Georgia, with a singular vision: to create handbags that celebrate timeless elegance, exceptional quality, and the spirit of the modern American woman.

Every design is sketched, prototyped, and hand-finished in our Buckhead atelier. We believe luxury should feel personal — each bag tells a story of artisan dedication and sustainable choices.

Our pillars

The WO Ethos

Three principles guide everything we create — from the first sketch to the final stitch.

Artisan Mastery

Each bag is handcrafted by master leather artisans with over 20 years of experience. We use traditional saddle stitching and edge painting techniques.

Sustainable Luxury

We source premium vegetable-tanned leathers from gold-rated tanneries and minimize waste. Our packaging is 100% recyclable and plastic-free.

Empowered by Women

Marias Handbag is female-founded and led. We champion diversity and partner with local women artisans to build an inclusive luxury future.

From Atlanta to the World

What started as a small studio in 2018 has grown into a beloved destination for women who refuse to compromise on style or substance. Our first collection featured just five silhouettes — today, we ship across the United States and have been featured in Vogue, Harper’s Bazaar, and The Atlanta Journal-Constitution.

We remain committed to slow fashion: producing limited quantities to honor craftsmanship and reduce overproduction. Each bag comes with a lifetime repair promise, because true luxury endures.
